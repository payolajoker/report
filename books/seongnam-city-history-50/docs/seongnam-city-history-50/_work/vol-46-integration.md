## Merge Candidates

### Index/README Summary

Vol46 is the elementary-school reader version of Seongnam City History. It compresses geography, city life, history, the 8.10 Seongnam/Gwangju Daedanji uprising, satellite/new-town growth, and cultural heritage into a workbook format with maps, activities, short narratives, and full-page visual learning materials. It is useful as a bridge between the specialized 50-year history volumes and educational/reproducible public-history content.
