## Merge Candidates

### Index/README Summary

Vol41 covers Moran permanent market and Moran folk five-day market as a combined urban traditional market. It is especially useful for linking Seongnam's rapid urbanization, the Gwangju Daedanji period, Moran pioneer narratives, stream-covering/market relocation, market modernization, merchant and visitor oral-history material, and literary representations of Moran Market.
