### A19-1,A20-1,A21-2 Block

- Left diagram caption: `생활가로변 시설배치계획`.
- Bottom image caption: `생활가로 모형`.
- Main rendering shows a street-edge residential/commercial frontage with lower podium-like uses and taller housing behind.

#### Community Layer

- The visible text describes the community layer as a buffer space between city and housing complex that remains active and functions like a light for the Pangyo residential complex.
- It also notes sectional planning using site-level differences and creation of a living street as an extension of the site.

#### 생활가로의 활성화

- The visible text describes the living street as a landmark-like active street and a buffer between city and residential complex.
- It provides culture, information, and sports services to residents and uses commercial facilities along the living street to create a lively street.
- The facility-placement diagram uses colored edge markers and small labels; fine labels are visually dense.
