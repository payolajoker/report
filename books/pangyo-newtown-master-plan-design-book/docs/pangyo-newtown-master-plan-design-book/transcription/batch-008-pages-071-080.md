### PAN GYO HOUSING Competition - A22-1,A22-2,A23-1 BLOCK

- Design office: `(주)무영 종합건축사사무소`.
- Listed names: 백시열 / 진하 / 임대규 / 박형진 / 이수진.
- Main concept statement: 본 계획의 가장 큰 특징은 `Multi-floor`, `Multi-class`, `Multi-funtion`이다.
- `Multi-floor`: 다층공간구조를 형성하는 것으로 입체적인 공간의 위계체계를 가지며, 보행네트워크가 입체적인 연계를 가지며 지형차를 고려한 데크형 주차시스템을 형성하게 된다.
- `Multi-class`: 다계층 욕구충족을 일컫는 말로서 다양한 소비자의 욕구에 대응한 단위공간계획을 가지며 라이프스타일에 대응한 가변형 공간구조로 어린이, 노약자를 위한 배려를 고려한 것을 말한다.
- `Multi-function`: 다기능 공간구조를 일컫는 말로서 입주민의 다양한 생활요구에 맞는 커뮤니티 공간으로 부지 주변 도시공간구조에 부합하는 프로그램을 설정하여 접근 및 이용의 편리를 배려한 보행 축변 집약배치를 말한다.
- Bottom concept thumbnails are captioned: `주동배치유형`, `데크시스템`, `외부공간계획`, `보행동선계획`, `차량동선계획`, `커뮤니티`.

### A22-1,A22-2,A23-1 Block

- Full-page open-space and pedestrian network diagram.
- Left vertical hierarchy labels: `중심 보행 가로`, `단지 녹지 중심`, `단위 공간`, `세대`.
- Plan labels include `STREET PARK(보행자전용도로)`, `POCKET PARK`, `EVENT PLAZA`, `WELL-BEING PROMENADE`, `PARK(SCHOOL PARK)`, `HERB GARDEN`, `STREET PARK`, `POCKET PARK`, `SCHOOL PARK`, `ECO-BRIDGE`, and `INFO NET`.

Readable plan notes:

- `STREET PARK(보행자전용도로)`: treats the city's main pedestrian axis as the primary access pedestrian-axis system, increases publicness through street-park design, links with a specialized commercial street, and uses event plaza, street exhibition, and flea-market programs.
- `POCKET PARK`: multifunctional park on natural ground, including children's playground, outdoor resident exercise facilities, and resting places; connects the public corridor with the semi-public park as the residential main-entry space.
- `EVENT PLAZA`: realizes pedestrian/vehicle separation through three-dimensional pedestrian paths, links spatially with the deck, connects to adjacent complexes by pedestrian overpass, and accommodates flea markets and various events as the central plaza.
- `WELL-BEING PROMENADE`: pedestrian greenway connected to the western neighborhood park; links entry plaza, outdoor stage, `SPORTS COMPLEX`, and `EVENT PLAZA`; supports walking, exercise, and event use; functions as the main approach route from public transit.
- `PARK(SCHOOL PARK)`: text is partly cut at the page edge, but visible notes describe active use of natural ground, biotope space, multi-layer planting/natural ecological space, and a nature-learning program linked to the school.
- `HERB GARDEN`: quiet garden formed on an artificial deck; small-scale community space with herb garden, tea garden, and communal garden.
