- Subheading: `MP업무`.
  - Advises and provides opinions for basic concept, development plan, implementation plan, district-unit plan, and housing construction business approval planning.
- Subheading: `운영방안`.
  - MP opinions are prioritized within the scope of applicable laws during permitting.
  - MP opinions are attached to basic concept, development, implementation, district-unit, and public-housing project approval applications.
