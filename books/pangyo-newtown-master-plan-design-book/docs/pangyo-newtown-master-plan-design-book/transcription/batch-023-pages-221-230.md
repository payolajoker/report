### 3.3. 건축계획

#### 3.3.1. 주거동계획

- Building form and placement should support natural resident exchange and contact.
- Common corridor/stair layouts should avoid infringing household privacy and support sunlight/ventilation.
- Terrace houses suited to ground contact are counted as first-floor placement.
- Outdoor access/approach planning is required for independent units on the third floor or higher.

#### 3.3.2. 단위세대계획

- Standard unit layout: 연립주택 `4LDK`, 테라스하우스 `4~5 LDK`.
- Unit heating: floor-heating structure.
- Master-bedroom attached spaces include bathroom, dressing room, powder room, and front balcony.
- Minimum visible ceiling height: `2.4m`.
- Duplex-type unit composition is permitted.

#### 3.3.4. 부대복리시설계획

Parking:

- Parking count: at least `2.4대` per household per block.
- Underground parking ratio, including deck parking, at least `90%`.
- Separate accessory-facility parking: one space per `150㎡`.
- Underground parking should be planned for direct natural lighting/ventilation where possible.
- Ramp internal turning radius: at least `5.5m`.
- Ramp slope: `14%` or less for curved ramps, `17%` or less for straight ramps.
