### PAN GYO HOUSING Competition: A17-1 Block

- Large title: `PAN GYO HOUSING Competition` and `A17-1 BLOCK`.
- Listed design offices and names:
  - `종합건축사사무소 시감` / `박용성`
  - `종합건축사사무소 명인` / `윤동현`
- Concept icon sequence: `AXIS`, `PEDESTRIAN`, `COMMUNITY`, `LANDMARK`, `SKYLINE`, `GREEN`.
- The main text summarizes three major planning ideas:
  - `Green (Eco-system & Green City)`: environmental protection/use of natural conditions, ecological environment creation, and a green network linking people, nature, and culture.
  - `Group (Urban Identity & Beauty)`: building placement responding to existing urban order and site shape, themed green spaces as community places, differentiated unit plans, and welfare facilities supporting wellbeing.
  - `Ground (Safe Community & Activity)`: reconnecting pedestrian flows with surrounding complexes, securing visual corridors and skyline composition, forming active community streets, and creating safe, pleasant pedestrian space.
- Bottom graphic strip uses green and black diagrams to move from `URBAN + Architecture` toward a people/nature theme; right-edge English text is partially cropped and not fully legible.
