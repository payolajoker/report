## PDF p.121

![PDF page 121](../assets/pages/pdf-page-121.jpg)

### PAN GYO HOUSING Competition: A2-2 BLOCK

- Architect/team: 종합건축사사무소 ITM.
- Names shown: 이한용 / 고봉철.
- Main concept heading: `'흐름의 완결'`.
- Legible concept summary: the site sits at a connection between the southern district living street, the northern natural neighborhood park, a central public pedestrian route, and the east-west pedestrian-only road. The plan emphasizes completing a circulation and landscape flow through a central public pedestrian passage, a green axis from the northern park, and a central node plaza.
- Stated themes include 자연의 향유, 너른 마당의 생성, and 대응 to the 생활가로, with district zoning and residential block character used to create a varied complex.
- Small perspective sketch shows a pedestrian street framed by apartment buildings.
- Bottom concept-strip captions: 가로의 형성; 구심적 광장의 형성; 녹의 유입; 단지의 연결.

### PAN GYO HOUSING Competition: A8-1 BLOCK

- Architect/team: 종합건축사사무소 ITM.
- Names shown: 이한용 / 고봉철.
- Main concept heading: `'흐름의 회복'`.
- Legible concept summary: the site is described as an "이음매" in the west Pangyo district circulation-street system. The proposal begins from the idea of naturally following the urban street-system flow, forming internal streets from existing street flows, and creating community cores at resulting nodes. The intended effect is to draw the district 생활가로 into the complex and connect the circulation loop.
- Small perspective sketch shows an apartment cluster with curving internal circulation.
- Bottom concept-strip captions: 가로의 형성; 단지 커뮤니티 코어; 생활가로의 연결; 자연요소의 유입; 단지의 완결성 부여.
