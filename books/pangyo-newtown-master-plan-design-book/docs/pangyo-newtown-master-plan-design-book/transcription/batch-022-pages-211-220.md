# B5-3 Block - 국제설계경기 프로젝트

Coverage note: PDF pages 211-220 document the `PANGYO HOUSING Competition` proposal for `B5-3 BLOCK`. The pages include project credits, competition statistics, land-use key map, master-plan diagrams, sustainability and slope-response concepts, community/parking plans, unit types A-D, landscape and water-system strategies, circulation hierarchy, and site sections. Review was visual only; small diagram labels and compact tables are summarized when not fully legible.

### Master Plan

- Top drawing: `Master Plan`, scale notation visible as approximately `Scale 1:2500`.
- Plan shows B5-3 as a long hillside site with contour lines, existing wooded areas, curving pedestrian routes, a blue watercourse, road access, and color-coded unit/building types labeled `A`, `B1`, `B2`, `C1`, `C2`, and `D`.
- Korean section heading: `경사에 따른 유형`.
- Text explains that three residential types are planned to respond to varied slopes and reduce site alteration: A+B for gentle slopes, C for steep slopes, and D on higher level ground.
- Bottom aerial rendering shows clustered housing stepping with the terrain, with darker forested slopes behind.

## PDF p.214

![PDF page 214](../assets/pages/pdf-page-214.jpg)

### 지속가능한 환경친화적 건축

- Top plan continues the master plan across the eastern portion of the site, with contour lines, north arrow, forest edge, road, clustered residential blocks, and yellow/orange/green building-type coloring.
- Korean text describes sustainable ecological housing:
  - Each dwelling has a private garden.
  - Green space is shared with neighbors.
  - Garden and cluster green space help reduce summer heat and provide psychological stability.
  - Four-story row housing on the north side blocks cold winter winds.
  - All units are planned to face south for even sunlight.
  - Building planning includes natural ventilation through natural air and stair systems, electric/gas heating systems, efficient roof sections, and broad grass roofs.
- Bottom rendering shows stepped low-rise housing and mid-rise blocks embedded in wooded terrain.

### 세대별 유형/A+B 타입

- Top diagrams: `Type A&B`, `Type A`, and `Type B`.
- Unit plan captions: `Type A`, `Type B-1`, and `Type B-2`.

### 세대별 유형/C+D 타입
