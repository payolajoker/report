![PDF page 257](../assets/pages/pdf-page-257.jpg)

Project/plan notes:

- New project heading: `PAN GYO HOUSING Competition A13-1,A14-1,B2-1 BLOCK`.
- Constructor line: `현대건설/한신공영/반도건설`.
- Firm: `(주)무영 종합건축사사무소`.
- Visible participant names: 안길원, 김영우, 김기백, 정미숙, 김성준, 최석용.
- The text defines two central themes: the apartment complex as an urban/nature-harmony landscape called `Panorama-Ring`, and the row-house complex as a nature-connected ecological complex called `Eco-Ring`.
- Apartment-site strategy: open arrangement from major urban viewpoints toward the foothill edge, securing views toward 금토산; a broad central plaza supports pleasant site environment; tower-type blocks toward the mountain slope and slab-type blocks along 생활가로 respond to the city.
- Row-house strategy: uses natural inflow and creates `Living Community`; site layout accepts urban flow naturally; creates ecological experience grounds extending from 금토산; four themed courtyards support community within the complex.
