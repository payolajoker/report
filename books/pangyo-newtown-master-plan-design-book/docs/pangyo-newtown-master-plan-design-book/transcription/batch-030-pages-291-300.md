- Main heading: `PAN GYO HOUSING Competition`.

  - `종합건축사사무소 명승건축(주)` with names visible as `이효주 / 이명원`.

#### Concept Summary

- The proposal states that the plan has two main characteristics.
- First, it actively accepts the surrounding `Nature` and `Urban` environments into the site and seeks harmonious integration. The text describes developing new residential-building types for each environmental condition. Landscape-view-oriented blocks are placed along the complex edge to maximize views, sunlight, and privacy, while wind paths are guided for a comfortable environment. In more urban cluster areas, view-corridor-responsive buildings create an urban environment within nature.
- Second, existing terrain and ecology are preserved and used, with community spaces planned around open spaces generated by site level differences. These spaces are intended to support varied outdoor activity and a lively residential landscape.
- The stated aim is a nature-friendly landscape apartment community where nature, city, and people harmonize, and where the plan enables a `코하우스 커뮤니티`.

#### Bottom Diagrams

- `CONTEXT` diagram with labels including `URBAN`, `NATURE`, `경관조망형 주동`, and `통경축대응주동`.
- `OPEN SPACE` diagram with `Natural open space` and `URBAN (Community) open space`.
- `COMMUNITY` diagram showing clustered building groups and circulation/community nodes; small labels are visually dense.
