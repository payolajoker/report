# A7-2 Block / 판교휴먼시아 아너스빌

Coverage note: PDF pages 311-316 document the A7-2 Block design for 판교휴먼시아 아너스빌 (6공구), including residential-type concepts, cluster courtyards, green and ecological landscape concepts, entry/landscape views, and community facilities. PDF pages 317-320 present unit-plan and interior-concept sheets for 101㎡, 115㎡, 153㎡, and 184㎡ homes.

Visible design narrative, summarized from the paragraph:

- The complex is described as part of the natural landscape, with low cluster organization intended to preserve surrounding mountain scenery and views.
- The village-center plaza is integrated with center-community functions.
- A community center is placed at the village's central point, compared with the traditional logic of placing a pavilion.
- Internal view corridors and walking paths are opened for hikers and neighboring residents.
- Warm nightscape lighting is planned to create a cozy nighttime atmosphere and social connection among residents.
