## PDF p.203

![PDF page 203](../assets/pages/pdf-page-203.jpg)

Top row shows communal deck and `shiki` room diagrams, including labeled use examples:

- `Home Bar`
- `Atelier`
- `Music Room`
- `Billiards`

Heading: `친수형 단지`

Readable planning notes:

- B5-2 block has two characteristic water-friendly open-space elements: `수로` and `비오톱 연못 네트워크`.
- `수로`: uses water flowing from the mountain to form a waterway.
- `Silty Sand` is noted as highly permeable; waterproof sheet or concrete is proposed at the waterway floor because of landslide concern.
- `연못네트워크`: biotope groups follow the waterway; waterproof sheet and pea gravel finish are noted for biotope floors.
- A visible quantitative note says `분당 5리터` of water flows into the biotopes, providing drainage/wetland conditions for plants and animals.

Large perspective caption: `친수공간과 보행녹도측에서 바라본 전경`.

Bottom section diagram: housing section with `shiki`, `bridge`, private gardens, communal deck/madang deck, roof garden, underground parking, cross-ventilation/privacy notes, and environmental devices. Some small technical labels are visually dense.
