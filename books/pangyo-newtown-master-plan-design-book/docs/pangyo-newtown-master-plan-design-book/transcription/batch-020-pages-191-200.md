## PDF p.200

![PDF page 200](../assets/pages/pdf-page-200.jpg)

- Visible headings/captions: `LAND USE KEYMAP`, `국제설계경기 프로젝트`.
- The keymap highlights the B5-2 location within the larger land-use plan.
- The main rendering shows low-rise clustered housing set into a green slope, with public paths and translucent human silhouettes.
- The lower green text explains three slope/orientation zones: gentle slopes `7~14°`, steep slopes `14~27°`, and north/west-facing slopes. It also describes clusters that bridge `3m`, `6m`, and `9m` level differences, excludes north/west slopes from building, and proposes two cluster types.
- Lower section captions: `Slope 7°~14°=Type A` and `Slope 14°~27°=Type B`. Small English labels include `room`, `shiki`, `communal deck`, `private garden`, and `parking`.
