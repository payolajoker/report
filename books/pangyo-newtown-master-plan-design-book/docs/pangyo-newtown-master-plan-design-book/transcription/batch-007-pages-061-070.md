# PDF pp.061-070 - 현상설계 프로젝트: A18-1 및 A5-1/A5-2 Blocks

Coverage: this batch continues `03 판교신도시 건축계획안`, `현상설계 프로젝트`, covering the A18-1 Block entry and the opening pages for A5-1/A5-2 Block. Notes below are based on visual page review only.

## PDF p.061

![PDF page 061](../assets/pages/pdf-page-061.jpg)

- Project heading: `PANGYO HOUSING Competition`, `A18-1 BLOCK`.
- Design office: `(주)종합건축사사무소 건원`.
- Listed names: `김종국 / 고성호 / 송현석 / 양남욱 / 이호훈 / 석장준`.
- Main text describes the site as bounded on the east by the Bundang-Suseogan expressway edge of Pangyo and on the west by a 25 m 생활가로.
- Planning concept responds to the regulated, repetitive high-rise edge along the expressway by opening voids near the lower part of A18-1 and the upper part of adjacent A18-2, separating portions by about 30 m and placing tower-type blocks toward the inside.
- West 생활가로 side is described as pedestrian-scaled: smaller, fragmented convenience facilities, varied forms/materials, and exposed corridors linking commercial/support facilities.
- Concluding concept phrase: west-side `Humanism Scale` and east-side `Urbanism Scale` coexist.
- Bottom perspective captions, left to right: `생태가로`, `중심가로`, `주보행네트워크`, `중앙광장`, `보행전용도로`, `진입가로`.

## PDF p.069

![PDF page 069](../assets/pages/pdf-page-069.jpg)

- Page header: `A5-1,A5-2 Block`.
- Main text explains that the two blocks face each other across a pedestrian road and are planned with a partly integrated single-block concept.
- Pedestrian road is treated as the organizing community axis, concentrating pedestrian circulation, support facilities, and outdoor spaces to create a differentiated community space.
- The two blocks use symmetry to support continuity of movement and linkage of support facilities.
- Landscape/skyline concept considers external arterial roads and city entry views, with a natural skyline connected to surrounding nature.
- Housing types mentioned: tower type, mid-rise slab type, and low-rise cluster residential buildings.
- Text emphasizes reducing the oppressive feel of high-rise apartments, maximizing directionality of visual corridors, preserving openness for views, and forming landscape through water axes and view corridors.
- Central small land-use image marks `A5-2` and `A5-1`; small zoning figures are too dense to treat as reliable.
- Bottom diagram captions: `커뮤니티 축생성`, `커뮤니티 중심생성`, `커뮤니티 활성화`, `열린 조망`.
